🧠 Map-Based Mnemonics

Divisions, borders aur districts yaad karne ki tricks

🧭 18 Mandal — West to East Trick
▶

West UP Mandals (5): Saharanpur → Meerut → Moradabad → Aligarh → Agra

Mnemonic

"Sundar Meerut Mahan Aligarh Agra" — Sab Mast Mast Aage Aao

Central/Awadh Mandals (3): Lucknow → Kanpur → Faizabad

Mnemonic

"Lucknow Ki Food" — LKF (Left to Right: Lucknow–Kanpur–Faizabad)

Terai Mandals (3): Bareilly → Devipatan → Basti → Gorakhpur

Mnemonic

"Bareilly Devi Basti Gayo" — BDBG

East UP Mandals (4): Azamgarh → Varanasi → Mirzapur → Prayagraj

Mnemonic

"Azam Varanasi Milaa Prayag" — AVMP

Bundelkhand Mandals (2): Jhansi + Chitrakoot

Mnemonic

"Jhansi ki Chitrakoot" — JC (Rani Laxmibai ki story yaad karo!)

🇳🇵 Nepal Border Districts (7) — Memory Trick
▶

West se East: Pilibhit → Lakhimpur Kheri → Bahraich → Shravasti → Balrampur → Siddharth Nagar → Maharajganj

Mnemonic (West→East)

"Phir Lo Bhaiyo Sabse Baadh Safar Mahan"
P=Pilibhit, L=Lakhimpur, B=Bahraich, S=Shravasti, B=Balrampur, S=Siddharth Nagar, M=Maharajganj

⭐ Sonbhadra — "4-State Wonder" Trick
▶

Sonbhadra UP ka eklauta district hai jo ek saath 4 states ko touch karta hai:

Trick

Sonbhadra → Madhya Pradesh + Chhattisgarh + Jharkhand + Bihar
"Sonbhadra Mega Corner Junction Border" = SMCJB

Also: Sonbhadra is UP's mineral-richest district — coal, bauxite, limestone, dolomite.
