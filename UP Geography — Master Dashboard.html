Bhautik Vibhajan

Physical Divisions of UP

Teen pramukh bhautik vibhag — Himalayan Terai, Ganga plain, Vindhya hills

1. Himalayan Terai & Bhabar region
Northern UP — Nepal border se laga
▼
Bhabar beltHimalayan foothills ke neeche — porous, boulder-laden. Rivers disappear underground here.
Terai beltSouth of Bhabar — waterlogged, marshy, dense forests. Rivers reappear.
DistrictsPilibhit, Lakhimpur Khiri, Bahraich, Shravasti, Balrampur, Siddharth Nagar, Maharajganj, Kushinagar
VegetationSal forests, tall grasses, dense jungles — Dudhwa National Park area
🌿 Economic importance: Sugarcane belt of UP — Lakhimpur, Bahraich sugar mills. Also major wheat & rice producer. Wildlife corridor — elephant, tiger passage.
⚡ UPPSC: Terai ki mitti = silty-clayey, highly fertile. Bhootar/bhurs (sandy mounds) bhi milte hain terai mein.
2. Ganga ka Maidaan (Indo-Gangetic Plain)
UP ka 90%+ area — sab se mahatvapoorn
▼
FormationTethys Sea ka talchhath — alluvial deposits by Himalayan rivers over millions of years
Sub-divisionsBangar (old alluvium, high ground) + Khadar (new alluvium, flood plains, fertile)
BhabarNorthernmost — coarse gravel, rivers go underground
TeraiRivers reappear — marshy, humid forests
Bhoor landSandy raised ground along river banks — found in Doabs
DoabLand between two rivers: Ganga-Yamuna Doab (most fertile)
Average elevation75m to 300m (slopes gently east-southeast)
Mitti prakarAlluvial soil (Domat) — most fertile in world for wheat & sugarcane

3. Vindhya + Kaimur Hills (Dakshini Pahari Kshetra)
Southern UP — Bundelkhand + Mirzapur–Sonbhadra belt
▼
Vindhya rangeJhansi–Lalitpur–Chitrakoot–Mirzapur–Sonbhadra. Hard red sandstone.
Kaimur rangeMirzapur–Sonbhadra — eastern extension. Coal-bearing. Limestone abundant.
BundelkhandGranite-gneiss rocky terrain — SW UP. Water scarcity chronic. Jhansi, Lalitpur, Mahoba, Banda, Chitrakoot, Hamirpur, Jalaun.
BaghelkhandSE extension — Mirzapur, Sonbhadra
Highest peak (UP)~1000m in Sonbhadra hills
⚡ Ken-Betwa River Link project: To solve Bundelkhand water crisis — India's first inter-basin river link. Approved 2021.
Minerals: Coal (Sonbhadra-Singrauli), Bauxite (Sonbhadra), Limestone (Sonbhadra-Mirzapur), Silica sand. UP's industrial belt.
⚠️ Bundelkhand problem: Less rainfall (<700mm), hard rock terrain, poor irrigation — droughts frequent. Ken-Betwa link solution.
