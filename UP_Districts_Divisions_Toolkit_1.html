🏆 UPPSC Key Facts: UP is India's most populous state (1st) and 4th largest by area. It has the highest number of Lok Sabha seats (80). Capital = Lucknow. Largest district by area = Lakhimpur Kheri (7,680 km²). Smallest district = Hapur (660 km²). Only district touching 4 states = Sonbhadra (MP, CG, Jharkhand, Bihar).

🏭 Famous Industries & Districts
Economic geography — UPPSC Paper 1 high-value topic
🎸
Carpet Weaving (कालीन उद्योग)
India's largest carpet export zone
Bhadohi (★ Carpet Capital) Mirzapur
Bhadohi (HQ: Gyanpur) = "Carpet City of India". Division: Mirzapur. Bhadohi aur Mirzapur milke India ke 80% handmade carpet export karte hain. GI tag: Mirzapur carpet. Exam trick: Bhadohi district Varanasi se alag hua 1994 mein.
👟
Leather & Footwear (चमड़ा उद्योग)
Asia's largest leather cluster
Kanpur (★ Leather City) Agra Unnao
Kanpur = "Manchester of the East" (textile+leather). Kanpur Nagar district, Kanpur Division. Agra = footwear (Bata, formal shoes). Unnao = tanneries. GI Tag: Agra Petha + Agra Leather Goods.
💻
IT / Tech Hub
NCR + Lucknow corridor
Noida (GB Nagar) Lucknow Ghaziabad
Noida = Gautam Buddha Nagar district, Meerut Division. IT companies + film city. Lucknow = IT/BPO growing hub. Ghaziabad = industrial corridor, NCR. UP Expressway Industrial Development Authority (UPEIDA) projects here.
🌿
Sugarcane / Sugar Mills (गन्ना उद्योग)
India's #1 sugarcane state
Muzaffarnagar Meerut Saharanpur Shamli Lakhimpur Kheri Bareilly Bijnor
Muzaffarnagar = "Sugar Bowl of India". UP produces ~40% of India's sugarcane. Western UP (Saharanpur, Meerut, Muzaffarnagar belt) = dense sugar mill zone. Doab region (Ganga-Yamuna) = fertile sugarcane land.
🔩
Brass / Metal Work (पीतल उद्योग)
World's brass hub
Moradabad (★ Peetal Nagari)
Moradabad = "Brass City of India" or "Pital Nagari". Moradabad Division, Paschim UP. World's largest brass handicraft exporter. GI Tag: Moradabad Metal Craft. Also famous for brassware export to USA, Europe.
🔒
Lock Industry (ताला उद्योग)
India's lock capital
Aligarh (★ Lock City)
Aligarh = India ka 80% lock production. Aligarh Mandal. Also famous for Aligarh Muslim University (AMU, 1875). GI Tag: Aligarh Lock.
✨
Glass / Bangles (काँच उद्योग)
Colourful glass industry
Firozabad (★ Glass/Bangle City) Varanasi (Silk Sarees)
Firozabad = "Suhag Nagri" - India's glass bangle capital (90% national production). Agra Division. Varanasi = Banarasi Silk Sarees (GI Tag). Varanasi Division, Poorvanchal.
🕌
Tourism Cities
UP = India's spiritual tourism hub
Agra (Taj Mahal) Varanasi (Ghats) Ayodhya (Ram Temple) Mathura-Vrindavan Prayagraj (Kumbh) Lucknow (Nawabi) Sarnath (Buddhist)
Prayagraj mein 2025 Maha Kumbh hua — temporary 76th district bana tha. Ayodhya = Ram Temple 2024 inauguration → religious tourism boost. Sarnath = Varanasi district mein, Buddha ka pehla pravachan. Kushinagar = Buddha mahaparinirvana.
⚗️
Chemical / Fertilizer
Heavy industry belt
Gorakhpur (Fertilizer) Sonbhadra (Power/Minerals) Kanpur (Chemical) Mathura (Refinery)
Mathura Refinery (IOC) = India ki sabse important oil refineries mein se ek. Gorakhpur Fertilizer Plant (revived 2022) = Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Ltd. Sonbhadra = Rihand Power Plant, Obra Power Plant + mineral wealth.
