🦏 Rhino Rewilding — Latest News (2024–2026)
🆕 2026

March 2026 Rhino Release: 4 greater one-horned rhinos (1 male + 3 females) were released into the wild at Dudhwa, taking free-ranging rhino population to 8. Third such operation after Nov 2024 and March 2025 (which released 4 more). Joint team of UP Forest Dept + WWF-India tracking via VHF and satellite signals. Goal: build stable breeding population in Terai landscape.

Source: The Better India, April 2026

🗺️ DTR Connectivity — Terai Arc Landscape

  • West: Pilibhit TR (contiguous forest corridor)
  • North: Bardia NP, Nepal (via Katerniaghat–Shuklaphanta corridor)
  • East: Sohagbarwa WS → Nepal forests
  • Part of WHO Global 200 Ecoregions; critical for tiger metapopulation

📌 Terai Arc Landscape (TAL) — Most Important for UPPSC

  • Stretches from Yamuna River (west) to Bagmati River (east)
  • Covers: Dudhwa TR, Pilibhit TR, Katerniaghat WS, Suhelwa WS, Sohagbarwa WS + Nepal's Bardia, Chitwan, Parsa NPs
  • One of WWF's Global 200 Priority Ecoregions
  • Supports: ~300 tigers, 2000+ Asian elephants, gharial, rhino, Gangetic dolphin
  • Fragmented by sugarcane fields, highways, agriculture — conservation challenge

🧠 Mnemonics for UP Wildlife

Exam mein yaad rakhne ke smart tricks

🐅 Tiger Reserve Order (Chronological)

"DAPR" = Dudhwa (1987) → Amangarh (2012) → Pilibhit (2014) → Ranipur (2022)

Sentence: "Dadi Aage Pe Ruki" (Dadi = oldest = Dudhwa; Ruki = Ranipur = youngest)

🦏 Dudhwa TR = 3 Parts

"DKK" = Dudhwa NP + Kishanpur WS + Katerniaghat WS

Rivers separating: Sarda = D↔K; Ghaghra = D↔Kater → "S before G" (Sarda = Kishanpur side, Ghaghra = Katerniaghat side)

💧 Ramsar Sites 2020 Batch (5 in one go!)

2020 mein 5 UP sites ek saath: "SPSNS" = Samaspur + Parvati Arga + Saman + Nawabganj + Sur Sarovar

Sentence: "Sab Parhte Saans Nahi Sochte"

🐊 Gharial kahan milta hai UP mein?

"CKH" = Chambal WS + Katerniaghat WS (Girwa River) + Hastinapur WS (Ganga stretch)

Gharial = CR species; Girwa River = Katerniaghat mein sirf gharial aur mugger ek saath milte hain (sympatric)

🗺️ North vs South UP Wildlife

NORTH (Terai belt): Dudhwa TR, Pilibhit TR, Amangarh TR, Suhelwa WS, Sohagbarwa WS, Katerniaghat WS, Kishanpur WS → All forest/grassland/wetland; Tigers + Rhino + Elephant

SOUTH (Vindhyan/Bundelkhand): Ranipur TR, Kaimoor WS, Ramsagar WS, Chandraprabha WS, Vijai Sagar WS → Dry deciduous; Leopard + Wolf + Chinkara

🌟 State Animals / Symbols

UP State Animal = Barasingha (Swamp Deer) = found in Dudhwa NP; Vulnerable species

UP State Bird = Sarus Crane = world's tallest flying bird; found in Ramsar wetlands of UP

Sarus Crane ke liye best zones: Saman BS, Samaspur BS, Nawabganj BS, Bakhira BS

📍 Location Trick: Ranipur kahan hai?

Ranipur TR = Chitrakoot district = Bundelkhand = SOUTH UP

Nearby: Panna TR (MP) = 150 km = same tigers come here; Vindhyan dry forest

Trick: "Rani Chitrakoot mein rehti thi" = Ranipur → Chitrakoot
