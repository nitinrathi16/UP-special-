01

Environment (Protection) Act

UPPSC ka favourite — iska year (1986) 2021 aur 2022 dono prelims me poocha gaya. "Umbrella legislation" kaha jaata hai.

🌍

Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 — EPA

1986
⚡ Trigger: Bhopal Gas Tragedy (Dec 1984) ke baad banaya gaya. Passed under Article 253 (international agreements — Stockholm Conference 1972 ko enforce karne ke liye).

Key Provisions

  • Umbrella Act: Central Govt ko sabhi environment-related matters par direct power deta hai.
  • Section 3: Centre standards set kar sakta hai, environmental authorities bana sakta hai (jaise CPCB ko direction).
  • Section 5: Centre ko direct power — kisi industry ko band/regulate karne ke directions (closure, prohibition).
  • Section 7: Prescribed standards se zyada pollutant emit/discharge karna prohibited.
  • Section 15: Penalty — up to 5 years jail + ₹1 lakh fine; continuing violation par extra.
  • Section 19: Koi bhi citizen 60-day notice ke baad court me complaint kar sakta hai (citizen suit).
  • EIA Notification (1994, 2006) aur CRZ Notification isi Act ke under nikalti hain.
🔄 Recent Amendment

Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Act, 2023 — minor violations ke liye jail ki jagah monetary penalty (decriminalisation) laaya; penalty range badh kar ₹5 lakh–₹15 crore tak; "Environmental Protection Fund" ka provision. Ease of doing business focus.

02

Forest Laws — Teen Stambh

1927 → 1980 → 2006: colonial control se conservation se community rights tak ka safar. Years ratta lagao.

🌲

Indian Forest Act

1927

Key Provisions

  • Colonial law — British timber/revenue control ke liye. Aaj bhi backbone.
  • Forests ki 3 categories: Reserved Forest (sabse strict), Protected Forest, Village Forest.
  • Reserved Forest me sab kuch prohibited jab tak permit na ho; Protected me sab allowed jab tak prohibit na ho.
  • Forest offences, transit of forest produce, duty on timber ka framework.
🪓

Forest (Conservation) Act

1980

Key Provisions

  • Maksad: forest land ka non-forest use rokna (deforestation control).
  • Koi bhi State forest land ko non-forest purpose (mining, dam, industry) me convert nahi kar sakta without prior Central Govt approval.
  • De-reservation of forests bhi Centre ki approval se.
  • T.N. Godavarman case (1996): SC ne "forest" ka dictionary meaning maana — sabhi recorded forests par Act lागू.
🔄 Recent Amendment

Forest (Conservation) Amendment Act, 2023 — Act ka naya naam "Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980". Preamble jhoda gaya. Strategic/security projects (100 km border ke andar), zoo, eco-tourism, safari ko clearance se chhoot. Aaya into force 4 Aug 2023. SC me challenge pending.

🪶

Forest Rights Act (Scheduled Tribes & OTFD)

2006

Key Provisions

  • Pura naam: Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006.
  • Forest-dwelling STs & OTFDs ko individual + community forest rights recognise karta hai (ye "historical injustice" sudhaarne ke liye).
  • Gram Sabha ko central role — rights ka determination aur consent.
  • Community right to Minor Forest Produce (MFP) — tendu, bamboo, etc.
  • Nodal Ministry: Ministry of Tribal Affairs (Forest Ministry nahi — yahi twist exam me aata hai).
🧭 Niyamgiri case (2013): SC ne Gram Sabha ko Vedanta mining project par decision ka adhikaar diya — FRA + community consent ka landmark.
03

Wildlife (Protection) Act

Schedules yaad rakho — 2022 amendment ke baad schedules ka structure badal gaya hai. Ye fresh exam material hai.

🐯

Wildlife (Protection) Act

1972

Key Provisions

  • Protected Areas ka framework: National Park, Wildlife Sanctuary, Conservation Reserve, Community Reserve.
  • National Board for Wildlife (NBWL) — PM chairman; Project Tiger / Project Elephant isi se jude.
  • CITES connection: WPA India ka domestic tool hai CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species, 1973) ko implement karne ke liye.

Schedules — Before vs After 2022

⏮️ Purana (6 schedules): Sch I & II = highest protection; Sch V = vermin; Sch VI = plants ⏭️ 2022 ke baad (4 schedules): Sch I = highest protection; Sch II = lesser; Sch III = plants; Sch IV = CITES-listed specimens
🔄 Recent Amendment

Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Act, 2022 — schedules 6 se ghata kar 4 kiye; CITES provisions formally add kiye (Management & Scientific Authority); invasive alien species par control; State Board for Wildlife ko Standing Committee. Penalties badhaayi.

⭐ Marks tip: "WPA 1972 me kitne schedules ab hain?" → 4 (2022 amendment ke baad). Purana answer "6" galat ho jaayega — yahi distinguishing point.
04

Water & Air Pollution Acts

Inhi do Acts se Pollution Control Boards (CPCB/SPCB) bane. Years: Water 1974, Air 1981 — sequence important.

💧

Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act

1974
  • India ka pehla comprehensive pollution control law. Article 252 ke under banaya (States ke request par).
  • Isi Act ne CPCB (Centre) aur SPCB (States) banaye.
  • Industries ko water me effluent discharge ke liye SPCB ka "consent" chahiye.
  • Saath me Water Cess Act, 1977 aaya (ab GST ke baad repealed).
🔄 Recent Amendment

Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Amendment Act, 2024 — kuchh categories ke liye consent requirement decriminalise/rationalise; minor offences par fine (jail ki jagah); Centre ko guidelines power.

🌫️

Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act

1981
  • Stockholm Conference 1972 ke decisions implement karne ke liye — Article 253.
  • Pehle se bane CPCB/SPCB ko hi air pollution control ke powers diye (naye board nahi).
  • SPCB "Air Pollution Control Areas" declare kar sakta hai; industries ko consent chahiye.
  • Noise pollution bhi (1987 amendment se) "air pollutant" me include.
🔗 Yaad rakho: Water (1974) ne Boards banaye → Air (1981) ne unhe extra kaam diya → EPA (1986) ne sab par umbrella daal di.

06

National Green Tribunal (NGT)

Statutory body (constitutional nahi). India teesra desh tha aisa tribunal banane wala — Australia & New Zealand ke baad.

🏛️

National Green Tribunal Act

2010
  • Jurisdiction: 7 environmental laws se jude "substantial questions" — Water 1974, Water Cess 1977, Forest Conservation 1980, Air 1981, EPA 1986, Public Liability Insurance 1991, Biodiversity 2002. (Wildlife Act 1972 aur Forest Rights Act 2006 NGT ke bahar hain — common trap.)
  • Principles: Polluter Pays, Precautionary Principle, Sustainable Development.
  • 6 mahine me case disposal ka target; CPC ke procedure se bandha nahi (natural justice).
  • Benches: Principal Bench New Delhi + Bhopal, Pune, Kolkata, Chennai.
  • Appeal sirf Supreme Court me, 90 din ke andar. 2021 me SC ne NGT ko suo motu power di.

⚖️ Landmark + Recent Judgments

2012
POSCO Steel (Odisha)

Environmental clearance suspend — forests & livelihood protect.

2013
Alaknanda Hydro (Uttarakhand)

"Polluter Pays" direct apply — flood damage ka compensation.

2015
Vardhaman Kaushik v. UOI

Delhi-NCR me 10 saal+ purane diesel vehicles ban.

2017
Almitra Patel (waste)

Open burning of waste par total prohibition — solid waste landmark.

2025 RECENT
Great Nicobar Project

₹72,000 cr transhipment project ki green clearances review ke liye NGT high-powered committee.

May 2026 LATEST
Sultanpuri Tree-Felling (Delhi)

Illegal felling par FIR + compensatory plantation ke directions (12 May 2026).

📌 Jan 2026 update: SC ne Article 142 ke tahत clarify kiya — NGT ke compensation awards decree ki tarah enforce ho sakte hain. Parliamentary committee ne WPA & FRA ko NGT jurisdiction me laane ki sifarish ki.
07

EIA & Coastal Regulation Zone

📋

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

1994 / 2006
  • EPA 1986 ke under notification. Pehla 1994, current framework EIA Notification 2006.
  • Process: Screening → Scoping → Public Consultation → Appraisal → Clearance.
  • Projects 2 categories: Category A (Centre — MoEFCC clears) & Category B (State — SEIAA clears).
  • Exemptions/trap: Category B2 projects ko EIA report & public hearing se chhoot. Draft EIA 2020 me aur exemptions propose hue (controversial).
⚠️ SC, Noble M. Paikada v. UOI: "ordinary earth" digging ke linear-project exemption ko SC ne strike down kiya — public consultation bypass nahi ho sakta.
🏖️

Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Notification

1991 → 2011 → 2019
  • EPA 1986 ke under. Pehla CRZ 1991, phir 2011, current CRZ Notification 2019.
  • Coast ko zones me baant-ta hai: CRZ-I (ecologically sensitive), CRZ-II (developed), CRZ-III (rural), CRZ-IV (water area).
  • 2019 me tourism/development ke liye norms ease hue; CZMA (Coastal Zone Management Authority) enforcement.
08

Biological Diversity Act

🦋

Biological Diversity Act

2002
  • CBD (Convention on Biological Diversity), Rio 1992 ko implement karne ke liye banaya.
  • Teen-star structure: NBA (National Biodiversity Authority — Chennai), SBB (State Biodiversity Boards), BMC (Biodiversity Management Committees — local level).
  • Focus: Access & Benefit Sharing (ABS) — bio-resources use se hone wale fayde ka local communities ke saath sharing.
  • People's Biodiversity Registers (PBR) — BMC banaate hain.
🔄 Recent Amendment

Biological Diversity (Amendment) Act, 2023 — AYUSH/registered practitioners ko ABS se chhoot; offences decriminalise (jail hata kar penalty); foreign investment process simplify. "Ease of business + traditional medicine" focus.

🎯 "Environmental Laws mein 5 guaranteed marks kaise loon"

SDM banne ke liye — ye 5 marks aapke control me hain. Concept nahi, sirf discipline chahiye.

  1. Years ratta = direct marks. Upar wali "Year Sheet" ko roz subah 2 min me dohraao. UPPSC ne EPA 1986 ka year 2021 aur 2022 dono me poocha — ye pattern hai, ittefaaq nahi.
  2. "Konsa Act kis cheez se bana" link banao. Water 1974 → Boards; Air 1981 → Stockholm; EPA 1986 → Bhopal; Biodiversity 2002 → CBD Rio. Cause-effect yaad rahega to confuse nahi hoge.
  3. Recent amendments alag se note karo. WPA schedules ab 4 (na ki 6), FCA ka naya naam, Jan Vishwas decriminalisation. Yahi se examiner "purana vs naya" trap banata hai.
  4. Nodal Ministry / chairman wale traps pakdo. FRA → Tribal Affairs (Forest nahi); NBWL → PM; NBA → Chennai. Ek-line factoids hi 1-markers ban-te hain.
  5. NGT ki jurisdiction yaad rakho — bahar kya hai. WPA 1972 & FRA 2006 NGT ke 7 Acts me nahi aate. Recent judgment (Great Nicobar 2025, Sultanpuri 2026) Mains me current-affairs flavour dega.
