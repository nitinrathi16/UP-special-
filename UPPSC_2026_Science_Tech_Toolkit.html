Key Facts — pehle yeh ratta maro

Har fact niche MCQs mein test hoga. Numbers aur years par focus — UPPSC yahi trap lagata hai.

🚀 ISRO 2025–26

  • 27 missions planned FY 2026-27; 4 Gaganyaan ke liye
  • Gaganyaan G1 (uncrewed): HLVM3 rocket + Vyommitra robot
  • H1 crewed 2027 — 3 astronauts; budget ₹20,193 cr
  • Bharatiya Antariksha Station target: 2035
  • PSLV-C62 (12 Jan 2026): EOS-N1 + 15 satellites — NSIL ka 9th commercial mission
  • NISAR = NASA-ISRO SAR, dual-band (L & S)
  • IRNSS-1F ne 10-saal mission life complete ki (NavIC: 7 satellites, ~1500 km coverage)

🎯 DRDO

  • RudraM-II: air-to-surface; Jun 2026 test, Su-30 MKI, ITR Chandipur
  • Astra: BVR air-to-air, 100+ km, indigenous seeker (Jul 2025)
  • Hypersonic missile: 1500+ km, Nov 2024, Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Island
  • ULPGM-V3: drone-launched PGM; RCI Hyderabad nodal lab (May 2026)
  • BrahMos → Philippines export Apr 2024; range 290 km
  • UP Defence Corridor: 6 nodes — Lucknow (BrahMos unit), Kanpur, Agra, Aligarh, Jhansi, Chitrakoot

⚛️ Nuclear Energy

  • Nuclear Energy Mission (Budget 2025-26): ₹20,000 cr; 100 GWe by 2047; 5 SMRs by 2033
  • SMR trio: BSMR-200, SMR-55, HTGC reactor (hydrogen)
  • SHANTI Act: nuclear sector mein private participation
  • PFBR Kalpakkam: criticality April 2026 — 3-stage programme ki 2nd stage
  • 3 stages: PHWR → Fast Breeder (Pu) → Thorium reactors

💾 Semiconductor · AI · Awards

  • ISM 2.0 (Budget 2026-27): ₹8,000 cr; equipment + materials + IP
  • Micron Sanand ATMP: inaugurated 28 Feb 2026 — pehli operational facility
  • Tata–PSMC Dholera fab: ₹91,000 cr; 28–110nm; first silicon Dec 2026
  • IndiaAI Mission: ~₹10,300 cr; MeitY; GPU compute + foundation models
  • Vigyan Ratna 2025: Prof. Jayant Narlikar (posthumous) — Hoyle-Narlikar theory
  • Vigyan Team 2025: CSIR Aroma Mission — Purple Revolution (lavender, J&K)

Section 03 · Forecast

UPPSC 2026 ke Top 5 Most Expected Topics

PYQ pattern + 2026 current affairs ke base par — yeh 5 topics non-negotiable hain.

1

Gaganyaan G1 + Vyommitra

Agar prelims se pehle launch hua, statement-based question almost guaranteed. Yaad rakho: HLVM3 rocket, Vyommitra robot, H1 = 2027 (3 astronauts), BAS = 2035, budget ₹20,193 cr.

2

PFBR Kalpakkam Criticality (April 2026)

Three-stage programme ke saath A-R ya statement question banega. Trap: PFBR 2nd stage hai, 3rd nahi. Thorium reserves (Kerala monazite) saath revise karo.

3

Nuclear Energy Mission + SHANTI Act + SMRs

Budget-linked schemes UPPSC ki favourite. Numbers: ₹20,000 cr, 100 GW by 2047, 5 SMRs by 2033, BSMR-200 / SMR-55.

4

UP Defence Corridor + BrahMos Lucknow

UP-specific angle = differentiator marks. 6 nodes ka matching question, Lucknow BrahMos unit (May 2025). RudraM-II 2024 mein aa chuka — 2026 test ke saath repeat possible.

5

Semiconductor Mission + Rashtriya Vigyan Puraskar 2025

"First operational facility = Micron Sanand" aur "Vigyan Ratna = Narlikar" — perfect single-statement MCQ candidates. Tata Dholera fab (₹91,000 cr) bhi note karo.

Topper Strategy

PYQ pattern se clear hai — UPPSC missile questions mein type (air-to-air vs air-to-surface) aur test month/year par trap lagata hai (RudraM-II 2024 ka question exactly yahi tha). Har defence system ke liye ek table maintain karo: type · range · launch platform. Science current affairs = differentiator marks for Top-10 rank.
